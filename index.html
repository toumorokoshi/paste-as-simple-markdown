About & Help

How to use

  1. Copy rich text (from Word, Google Docs, etc.) or LaTeX equations.
  2. Paste directly into the left "Input" pane.
  3. The app automatically converts the content to clean Markdown and translates LaTeX to Unicode.
  4. Click "Copy Markdown" or "Copy Plaintext" to get the result.
